What is the latest place that I have visited?

New York is a stunning city that stole a piece of my heart.
The most memorable experience in the "Big Apple" for me was the view from the Empire State Building in the night-time.
The blend of modern buildings together with traditional old-style Dutch architecture creates a peculiar charm for the city.
If you ever get to visiting this place, make sure you visit the Central park and enjoy a cup of fresh coffee while sightseeing in there ;)
